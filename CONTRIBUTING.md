# Contributing

Thanks for improving these public CRA Direct docs.

## Scope

Good contributions include:

- clearer technical explanations
- corrected examples
- improved operational checklists
- safer wording around Cyber Resilience Act readiness
- links to public standards or specifications

Out of scope:

- legal advice
- claims of guaranteed compliance
- private customer data
- unpublished regulator correspondence
- secrets, service keys, or production incident data

## Writing Style

- Be concrete and operational.
- Prefer "helps", "supports", and "readiness" over absolute compliance claims.
- Distinguish facts, assumptions, and implementation choices.
- Keep examples fake but realistic.
